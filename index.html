Learning Hub

Understand the fundamentals

Learn the terms and concepts commonly used in Dubai’s property market before making an investment decision.

Off-Plan Property

A property purchased before construction is completed. Buyers normally follow a payment plan linked to construction milestones.

Ready Property

A completed property that can normally be occupied or rented immediately after ownership transfer.

Rental Yield

Rental yield compares annual rental income with the property’s purchase price and is shown as a percentage.

Service Charges

Recurring fees collected for building maintenance, security, shared facilities and community management.

Payment Plans

A schedule explaining when portions of the property price must be paid before, during or after construction.

Capital Appreciation

The increase in a property’s market value between its purchase date and a later sale or valuation date.
